Ma100 Augmented Reality app

Context

My school asked me and 5 other people if we would like to create an augmented reality application in celebration of the 100th year anniversary of the school itself.
When the app is running, hold it in front of an specific image (this would eventually be the cover of the 100th anniversary book.) and an circular menu would popup with 3D objects relevant to the history of our school.
When one of these objects is clicked it would move to the foreground and you could inspect it whilst also being able to read some information regarding it.

With who?

I worked on this project with 2 other programmers and 3 other artists. Once more I assumed the position of team leader and primarily designed how the app would function. Furthermore helped with the programming, especially with the AR part but I did not program directly.

When

I have worked on this project for about 35 hours over a period of 6 weeks.

Why

I wanted to get a bit familiar with augmented reality and to get more experience working on a commissioned project.

What did i learn?

How to work with AR in unity on a basic level.
